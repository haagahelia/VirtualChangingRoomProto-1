Magic Fitting Room

How to use the Magic Fitting Room!

1. Add a picture of yourself by clicking the image button (Vertical images are better)

2. Add clothes by clicking the clothes button and then selecting an element.
You can choose between Water, Fire, Wood, Metal and Earth.
Then click on the items you want, and position them over your picture

3. Download a copy of your final image by clicking the download button.
The image will download into your download folder. (This might look different from the example video in your own browser)

4. Click the trash can button to remove your picture.

We care about your privacy!

Application is collecting anonymous visitor data for research purposes.
The study is being carried out by Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences as part of a wider development project supported by the European Regional Development Fund in the Future Touchless Shop project. The data collected for the study will only be available to the researchers of the project and will be destroyed no later than September 1, 2024.
